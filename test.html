    Image Based Icons

    These icons are based on background images and image sprites and not the icon font. The drawback to this approach is that the icon can not be resizedor colored differently with standard mix-in classes.

    Specialized Icons

    There are twenty-seven (27) specialized icons available for use, including twenty (20) map pin icons and two (2) social media icons. These icons are unique in that they cannot be embedded on the page using a single class, but rather rely on having a series of child <span> tags with a class of "path#" where "#" is replaced with incrementing numbers starting at 1. These icons are built this way because they have multiple colors or strokes of varying opacity. For example, all of the document icons use this specialized method since the icon contains both color strokes as well as black strokes.
